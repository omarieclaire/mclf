Speculation

Speculation is a theatrical concert with an immersive visual design based on the experience of vision loss.

Tonight was the opening night for Leslie Ting's beautiful work *Speculation* at @beyondwallsTPM.

I helped with the experience design for a live-streamed phone-in performance of 4'33". My favourite unexpected sound was a cat making an adorable meow. https://t.co/sUYv8Y7AEx

— Marie LeBlanc Flanagan (@omarieclaire) March 18, 2021
