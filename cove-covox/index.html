COVE/COVOX
Inter Arts Matrix

COVE/COVOX: COVE is a project funded primarily by the Ontario Trillium Foundation with the goal of transmitting knowledge to the next generation of artistic directors. COVOX is a project funded by Canada Council for the Arts Digital Strategies Fund, and builds on COVE with the goal of experimenting, developing, and sharing knowledge about digital audience engagement with small and medium-sized arts organizations in the Waterloo Region.

COVE/COVOX supported six artist mentees as they incubated and developed interdisciplinary projects and performances. For the spring season of X-Camera talks, we focused each week on a different artist mentee who presented their artistic practice and projects.

Partner organizations for COVE/COVOX include MT Space, Open Ears Festival of New Music & Sound, Numus Concerts, Studio D20 Women's Media Art Collective, Neruda Productions, and Idea Exchange | Cambridge Galleries.
