Tiny Dance Closet

I made the Tiny Dance Closet in collaboration with students and faculty at the NYU Game Center.

It was absolutely tiny, just big enough for one person. The project included stanchions to manage the lineup, a bouncer, hand stamps, black velvet curtains, a personal dj, a light show, and projected visuals.
