Document Your Work

Document Your Work was a 10 workshops from August 31 2020, and June 15 2022

Are you always making something new? Are you a wild tumble of creativity and creation, swooping and rolling through existence? Are you trailed by a scattering of gorgeous debris? Do you have an external hard drive called "hoard hole" full of half-written project descriptions, photos, and videos? Are there divine secrets in your past work waiting for your thoughtful re-exploration to emerge? Are the machinated bureaucrats and measuring-tape blob-lords withholding all the treasures until you polish your portfolio or website?

Come join us and document yourself into existence!

Structure:

  • Introductions / sharing what we each will work on (10 minutes and totally optional)
  • DOCUMENT (solo work - 90 minutes)
  • Sharing (~15 minutes and totally optional)

Just finished the 7th "Document Your Work!" session

It's 90 mins of silent co-supportive work: documenting projects, updating your site, writing a bio, updating your cv, or anything that feels like documentation to you
