Play with glowing orbs at dusk

Join us for an evening of ritual and play at Frédéric Back Park.

Frédéric Back Park sits on 40 million tonnes of material. On the surface, you'll find mysterious orbs, venting ports for an active process happening beneath our feet. As the sun sets, we'll paint with light on the orbs.

What to expect

Experimental play at dusk, exploring trash, territory, and transformation. Bring a flashlight or bike light and dress warm.

We'll begin with a mediative ritual as the sun sets, and shift into faster-paced play in the late dusk.
