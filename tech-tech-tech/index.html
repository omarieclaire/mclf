Tech Tech Tech- why not tech giants?
Research project with Ada X Montreal

Exploring alternatives for office tools, social media, online gatherings, portfolios, performances & exhibitions

Working on an amazing research project with @ada_x_mtl exploring alternatives to the tech giants

We just wrapped up a 4-part workshop with co-researchers exploring alternatives for office tools, social media, online gatherings, portfolios, performances & exhibitions

— Marie LeBlanc Flanagan (@omarieclaire) December 17, 2021

Things I love about this project:

  • The team is amazing. Everyone I'm working with is brilliant and kind
  • I feel like I'm starting to reshape my perspectives around questions of accessibility, privacy, consent, flow/friction, speed, reliability, and ecological impact

Things I don't love:

  • It feels like every tool comes with some trade-offs. It's hard for a community-made tool to match the stability and accessibility features of the giants
  • I'm thinking about these questions all the time on every tool I use and it's a bit exhausting
