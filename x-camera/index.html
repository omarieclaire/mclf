X-Camera
presented by COVE/COVOX and Inter Arts Matrix

X-Camera: An Experimental Learning Circle is a weekly lecture series by artists for artists. It began in the Spring of 2019 when a group of people interested in the arts began gathering at Fresh Ground Café/Commons Studio in Downtown Kitchener every Friday afternoon to hear a lecture by an interdisciplinary artist or professional. The lectures were followed by a time for people to stay in the café space, eat lunch together, continue conversations and build a community. This happened every Friday afternoon until March 2020 when these gatherings shifted online.

I hosted the spring season of X-Camera talks. We focused each week on a different artist mentee from the COVE/COVOX program, who presented their artistic practice and projects.

Sam Mercury: mixed(er)

Grace Scheele: not yr angel bby harpist
