Long Winter

Long Winter is a landmark music and arts monthly showcase in Toronto. Long Winter delivers an inspired experience of diverse, far reaching, cutting-edge live arts such as music, visual art, large scale installation, video, performance art, theatre, dance, new media and more recently xR. Each season, our series offers an eclectic one off, “something-for-everyone” experience in Toronto’s colder, quieter winter season.

Long Winter was created with the artists: Shonee (Bianca Shonee Arroyo), Cheyenne Rain LeGrande ᑭᒥᐊᐧᐣ, Kasra Goodarznezhad, Malik McKoy, and Vasuki Shanmuganathan.
