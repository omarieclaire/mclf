Parallel Park Play

A tiny gathering for us to play alongside each other.

You might: sketch, write poems, embroider, bead, sculpt miniatures, bead, play with electronics, make up stories, weave something with park twigs, daydream, something else? Each person can do something that feels nice for them.

You can talk to the people close to you, listen to conversations, or you can immerse yourself in your play and not talk at all, all are welcome.

Bring a blanket to sit or stretch out on, this is a distanced outdoor hang. We'll be nearest the Laurier/Mentana corner. My number is 438 346 8025 if you get lost and want help.

If you can't come, don't worry, I'll just invite you another time.
